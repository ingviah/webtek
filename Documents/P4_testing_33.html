Tester Audience

The intended users were players of Nonstop FK as well as the supporters of the team. Therefore, all of our testers were of a similar age and interest group. All of our testers were students, and are interested in finding out more about Nonstop FK, and information that the website has to offer.

Testing Platforms

The tests where mainly performed on computer, with various browsers. Mainly Google Crome was used to conduct the tests

Feedback summary

Summary positive feedback:

  • Ease of navigation: testers found the website very easy to navigate, with essential features like the table and latest articles being quickly identifiable and accessible.
  • Clear and clean design: Many testers appreciated the clean, simple, and organized layout, which made the site visually appealing and user-friendly.
  • Relevant and comprehensive content: Testers noted that the website provided more detailed information compared to other platforms like Instagram, with an appreciated overview of past matches and league standings.
  • Intuitive user interface: Features like the news articles being pop-ups instead of redirecting to a new page, and the ability to return to the homepage by clicking the logo, were well-received for enhancing user experience.

Summary negative feedback:

  • Lack of clarity in section titles: Some testers found the naming of sections like "kampoppsett" confusing, suggesting more intuitive titles for better understanding.
  • Feedback mechanism needs improvement: Testers suggested that the text in the feedback submission box should disappear after submission to avoid confusion.
  • Lack of differentiation in homepage elements: Testers observed that various elements on the homepage seemed to blend together, recommending clearer separations or framing to distinguish between articles, tables, and upcoming matches.
  • Need for more detailed information: Suggestions included adding dates to news articles, clearer distinctions in the league table standings, and providing more in-depth information about the players and revent matches.
